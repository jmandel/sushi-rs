http://hl7.org/fhir/us/core/StructureDefinition/us-core-familymemberhistory-recorder

Individual who recorded the family member's health history and takes responsibility for its content. Because the recorder takes responsibility for accurately recording information in the record, the recorder is the most recent author. The recorder might or might not be the asserter.

This profile builds on Extension.

This profile was published on 2025-09-28 as a active by HL7 International / Cross-Group Projects
